Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Description

Pasta al pomodoro is a classic Italian dish. It’s simple yet full of flavor, perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or fusilli)
  • 400g canned peeled tomatoes (or tomato puree)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated Parmigiano Reggiano (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of salted water for the pasta.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil and add the crushed garlic cloves. Sauté until the garlic turns golden.
  3. Add the peeled tomatoes (or tomato puree) to the pan, season with salt, and cook on medium-low heat for about 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  4. Cook the pasta in the boiling water according to the package instructions.
  5. Drain the pasta al dente and add it directly to the pan with the sauce. Stir well to combine.
  6. Serve the pasta al pomodoro with fresh basil leaves and a sprinkle of Parmigiano Reggiano if desired.
