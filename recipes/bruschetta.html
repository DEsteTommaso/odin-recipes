Tomato Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta al pomodoro is a simple and tasty appetizer, perfect for starting a meal or a quick snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of rustic bread
  • 3 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Toast the bread slices on a grill or in a toaster until golden and crispy.
  2. Dice the tomatoes and place them in a bowl.
  3. Season the tomatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, and some torn basil leaves.
  4. Rub the garlic clove on the toasted bread slices for added flavor.
  5. Spoon the seasoned tomatoes onto the toasted bread.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and, if desired, a sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper.
